About RentorBuyCalculator.net

RentorBuyCalculator.net is a simple tool that helps you compare the long-term cost of renting versus buying a home.

Why this calculator exists

Many people make the rent vs buy decision based only on the monthly payment. In reality, there are many other costs: interest, property taxes, maintenance, insurance, and rising rent. This calculator brings those numbers together so you can see the bigger picture.

How it works

  • Estimates total rent paid over time, including yearly rent increases.
  • Calculates mortgage payments for your chosen interest rate and loan term.
  • Adds approximate property tax and maintenance percentages.
  • Shows which option is cheaper over your selected time horizon.

Who is behind the site?

This project is run by an independent developer who enjoys building practical finance tools. The goal is to keep the calculator fast, free, and easy to use.
