About RentorBuyCalculator.net

A simple, transparent way to compare the long-term cost of renting versus buying a home.

What is this website?

RentorBuyCalculator.net is a free, independent web tool that helps you compare the long-term cost of renting versus buying a home. Instead of guessing based on only the monthly payment, the calculator includes key factors like:

  • Monthly rent and expected yearly rent increases
  • Home price, down payment and mortgage interest rate
  • Property taxes, maintenance and insurance estimates
  • Total cost of renting and buying over your chosen time horizon

The goal is to give you a clear, numbers-based view of each option so you can make your own informed decision.

Who is behind the site?

RentorBuyCalculator.net is a small, independent project created by a solo developer who enjoys building practical tools for everyday financial decisions. There is no real-estate company, bank, or broker sponsoring this calculator.

Because this is an independent project, the calculator is designed to be:

  • Neutral: it does not push you toward renting or buying.
  • Simple: only the most important inputs are required.
  • Transparent: assumptions are visible and editable by you.

Important disclaimer

This website does not provide financial, tax, or investment advice. The calculator is a general educational tool based on simplified assumptions and public information. Your real situation may differ significantly based on your market, income, credit profile and tax situation.

You should always consult with a qualified professional (such as a financial advisor, mortgage specialist or tax advisor) before making major financial decisions.
